# Churn Rate Analysis

## Project Overview

### Dataset Description

The Telco Customer Churn dataset contains information on customers' churn details, including their customer ID, gender, age, seniorcitizen, partner, dependents, tenure,	phone service, multiplelines, internet service,	online security, online backup,	device protection, tech support, streamingTV,	streaming movies, contract, paperless billing, payment method, monthly charges, total charges and churn. The dataset is focused on the Telecommunications activities of Telco customers.

### Objectives (Business problem):
* Analyze the churn distribution based on payment method of Telco customers and identify their count on each of these payment methods.
* Compare total and monthly charges based on whether customers churn or not.
* Analyze the tenure churn count. 
* Analyze the churn of customers based on contract type.
* Report your findings based on the information above.

### Data Preparation (data cleaning, structure, and model):

* Load your data from from different sources and establishes the foundation for your data model.
* Combine Data (Joins, Relationships, Blends)
* Clean & Transform Data; rename columns change data types, fix incorrect values or nulls, split or merge columns.
* Create Calculated Fields
* Apply Aggregations & Hierarchies


### Data View 
* Displayed the churn per different payment methods (Bank Transfer, Credit Card, Electronic Check and Mailed Check) and their respective counts on each using cards.
* Displayed total vs monthly charges using scatter visuals.
* Added a tenure churn count using stacked bar charts.
* Showed the churn based on contract type with their respective counts using a stacked bar charts.
